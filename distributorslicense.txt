Construction and Distribution Contract (The Computre Type Foundry) (11/12/17)

- "DESIGNER" refers to The Computre Type Foundry. "DISTRIBUTOR" refers to the font seller / distributor.

- Any request from the "DESIGNER" regarding the typeface or font family will be carried out by the "DISTRIBUTOR" immediately.

- The "DISTRIBUTOR" does not own any creation from the "DESIGNER", and will only recieve money that is decided by both parties.

- Any violation of these set rules will result in the revoking of distribution privillages for products of The Computre Type Foundry.
